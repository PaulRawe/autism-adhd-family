Energy Scale

1

Energy Scale ‚Äì The Card

Shows how much strength or energy is currently available

When is this scale useful?

  • After school, daycare, or care settings
  • During fatigue or exhaustion
  • During withdrawal or becoming quiet
  • Before or after transitions

How is it used?

  • The child shows the level that feels right
  • No explanation is necessary
  • The displayed state is not commented on or evaluated

Important to know

  • Low energy does not mean lack of motivation
  • Low energy often requires reduction, not activation
